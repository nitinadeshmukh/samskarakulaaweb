## 1. This is our computed version

The Kundali shown in the app is **Samskara Kula's own computed version**. It is
generated from astronomical ephemeris data using the sidereal (Lahiri ayanamsha)
system to calculate planetary positions and the ascendant for the birth details
you provide. It is produced by software, **not** by a human astrologer, and is
offered for your **reference and convenience only**.

Different astrologers, traditions, ayanamsha systems, and software may produce
**different charts and different interpretations** from the same birth details.
Our computed chart is **one** such version and is **not** an authoritative,
official, or professional astrological reading, and is **not** a substitute for
consultation with a qualified astrologer (Jyotishi) or priest.

## 2. Upload your own kundali

Because interpretations legitimately differ, you may **upload your own kundali**

**do not** use it in our calculations. Where our computed chart and your uploaded
chart differ, **your own trusted sources take precedence** — our version does not
override them.

## 3. Accuracy depends on your inputs

The chart is only as accurate as the birth details you enter. In particular:

- **Birth time** materially affects the ascendant and house positions. If you
  don't provide an exact birth time, the app clearly flags **reduced precision**
  and does not fabricate a time — but the resulting chart should be treated as
  approximate.
- **Birth location** affects the calculation; an imprecise city or coordinates
  reduces accuracy.
- We **never** guess or default missing birth time, Gotra, Veda/Shakha, or Kula
  Devata. Missing values are shown as "unknown", not filled in.
