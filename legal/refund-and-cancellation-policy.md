cancel and what has already been provisioned.

booking reference. The cancellation takes effect when we receive it; timing is
measured against the ceremony's scheduled date/time.

## 3. Samagri and Prasad (physical goods)

3.1 **Samagri** (ritual materials shipped before a ceremony): once a Samagri kit
has been dispatched, its cost is **non-refundable**, because it is prepared/
assembled for your specific ceremony. If it has **not** yet been dispatched, its
cost is refundable per the table in §2.

3.2 **Prasad** (consecrated offerings shipped after a ceremony): being consecrated
and perishable, Prasad is **non-refundable** once prepared or shipped.

3.3 **Shipping, customs, and cross-border:** shipping fees, duties, and taxes are
generally non-refundable once incurred. International delivery times, customs
clearance, and carrier handling are outside our full control; delays caused by
carriers or customs do not by themselves entitle you to a refund of the service
fee, though we will help resolve delivery problems.

3.4 **Damaged / lost / wrong item:** if physical goods arrive damaged, are lost in
transit, or are materially not as described, contact [SUPPORT EMAIL] within [7]
days with photos/details. We will arrange a replacement or refund of the affected
item as appropriate.

## 4. Cancellation or failure by the Priest / by us

4.1 If a Priest cancels, fails to appear, or cannot perform a confirmed ceremony,
you are entitled to a **full refund of the service fee** for that ceremony, or, at
your option, help re-booking with another available Priest.

4.2 If a **virtual** ceremony cannot go ahead because of a failure on our side
(e.g. the stream link is never generated), you are entitled to a full refund of
the service fee. Interruptions caused by **your** device, network, or a third-party
service are not our responsibility (see Terms §4.3).

4.3 If **we** cancel a booking (e.g. for safety, legal, or operational reasons),
you receive a full refund of amounts paid for the cancelled ceremony, less any
non-refundable physical goods already dispatched.

## 5. Rescheduling

Where possible, you may reschedule rather than cancel, subject to Priest
availability and a valid Muhurta window. Rescheduling requests made [more than 48
hours] before the scheduled date are normally accommodated without penalty;
later requests are treated as a cancellation under §2.
